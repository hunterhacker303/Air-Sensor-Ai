Air Quality Index

The Air Quality Index (AQI) in major cities across India varies widely due to industrial activities, vehicle emissions, dust, and seasonal changes. Cities like Delhi often experience severe pollution during winter, causing health risks and visibility issues. Continuous monitoring of AQI helps in taking preventive measures, improving policies, and making cities cleaner and safer for the public.
